About Us

S.V Engineering was established in 2005 as a Proprietorship company having manufacturing location at Odhav, Ahmedabad in Gujarat State. The company is certified by M/s BSCIC Certification Pvt. Ltd. The company is manufacturing and capable of supplying all type of Machine part, Spare of Gauge & Fixture.

The company has shown consistent improvement in its performance over the years in the automotive segments & is poised for good growth.

QUALITY POLICY

At S.V Engineering we shall strive for Total Customer Satisfaction by supplying them Quality Turned Components within committed time frame. To achieve this continual improvement philosophy will be adhered to.

QUALITY OBJECTIVE

  • To Enhance Customer Satisfaction
  • To ensure timely deliveries to customers.
  • To reduce wastages in our manufacturing operations.
  • To minimize Non conforming products by effective monitoring of processes
